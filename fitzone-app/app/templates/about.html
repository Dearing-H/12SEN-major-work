About FITZONE

FITZONE is an AI-powered training dashboard built to help users track workouts, calculate BMI and daily calories, and monitor fitness progress with real-time feedback. Designed with a sleek, modern interface, the app prioritizes both functionality and user experience.

About the Developer

My name is Harrison Dearing, and I'm a Year 12 student passionate about both fitness and technology. I built FITZONE as part of my Software Engineering Major Work to combine my technical skills with a real-world application that can genuinely help people take control of their health journey.

Key Features

  • Smart BMI & TDEE Calculators
  • Daily logging for meals, water, and reps
  • Workout planning and goal setting
  • Progress charts and summaries
  • AI-generated workout and meal plans

Inspired By

This project draws inspiration from apps like MyFitnessPal, Strong, and Fitbod, aiming to offer a seamless all-in-one fitness solution with a personal touch.
