About FITZONE

Fitzone is a modular, interactive fitness dashboard designed as part of the Year 12 Software Engineering Major Work. Built with Python, Flask, and HTML5/CSS3, it aims to centralise health metrics, workout planning, and user progress tracking into one user-friendly platform.
