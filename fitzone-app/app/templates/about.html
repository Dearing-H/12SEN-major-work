About FITZONE

FITZONE is a passion project developed by me, Harrison Dearing, as part of my Year 12 Software Engineering Major Work. Designed with a clear focus on user empowerment and data-driven fitness, FITZONE integrates cutting-edge web technologies including Python, Flask, HTML5, and CSS3 to provide an intuitive and motivating health companion.
